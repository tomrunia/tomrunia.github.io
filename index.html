About Me

I am Tom Runia, a PhD student at the University of Amsterdam working on Deep Learning under the supervision of professor Cees Snoek and Arnold Smeulders. Before coming to Amsterdam, I obtained a BSc. degree in Applied Physics and MSc. degree in Computer Science, both from Delft University of Technology. For my Master thesis I did an internship at TomTom research lab in Eindhoven, where I worked on accelerating object detection algorithms for autonomous driving applications. My main research interests are in video understanding, action recognition and deep learning. This year I am supervision the Deep Learning course at the University of Amsterdam. Motivated students are welcome to drop me an e-mail about project supervision.
